Regression

Prediction Equation

$$\hat{y} = a + bx$$

Ordinary Least Squares Calculations

$$b = \frac{\sum\left(x - \bar{x}\right)\left(y - \bar{y}\right)}{\sum\left(x-\bar{x}\right)^2}$$
$$a = \bar{y} - b\bar{x}$$

Correlation

Definition

  • Correlation, r, is a standardized version of the slope in the regression equation
$$r = \left(\frac{s_x}{s_y}\right)b$$
